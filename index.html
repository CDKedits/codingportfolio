About

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Connor Nguyen. I am a full stack developer, video editor, and former professional gamer. Prior to my career as a full stack developer, I worked as a video editor focused on creating content for various companies within the gaming industry.

Then, I made one of the best decisions of my life and discovered my passion for coding by attending the Coding Boot Camp at University of California, Irvine! Through this rigorous program, I gained an immense amount of experience creating web applications with popular programming technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Node.js, and React.

As a graduate of the program, my main focus is to create full-stack applications with an emphasis on having clean designs that will provide a simple and pleasant experience for users.
